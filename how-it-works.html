How Boomer Ask Works

What is Boomer Ask?

Boomer Ask is a friendly way to get information from artificial intelligence (AI) technology. We've designed it specifically for seniors who want clear, reliable answers without the confusion of complicated technology.

Think of Boomer Ask as a helpful research assistant who can quickly find information for you and explain it in a clear, straightforward way.

How Does AI Work?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is like a very smart computer program that can understand questions, search for information, and provide answers in plain language.

Here's a simple explanation of how it works:

  1. Learning: The AI has been trained on millions of books, articles, and websites to learn facts and information.
  2. Understanding: When you ask a question, the AI tries to understand what you're asking about.
  3. Searching: It searches through all the information it has learned to find relevant answers.
  4. Answering: It creates a response in clear, everyday language that directly answers your question.

The AI is designed to be helpful, accurate, and easy to understand. It's not a person, but a tool created to assist you with finding information.

Using Boomer Ask: Step by Step

1

Ask Your Question

Type your question in the search box on the home page. You can ask about almost anything - health information, technology help, current events, recipes, or advice.

If typing is difficult, click the "Use Voice Instead" button and speak your question clearly.

Example Questions:
  • "How do I video chat with my grandchildren?"
  • "What are the side effects of ibuprofen?"
  • "How can I protect myself from online scams?"
  • "What's a good recipe for diabetic-friendly apple pie?"
2

Get Your Answer

After you submit your question, Boomer Ask will think for a moment and then provide an answer. The answer will be:

  • Written in clear, easy-to-understand language
  • Organized with helpful headings and bullet points
  • Based on reliable information sources
  • Honest about what it knows and doesn't know

At the bottom of each answer, you'll see the sources of information used, so you know where the information came from.

3

Follow Up If Needed

If the answer isn't clear or you want to know more, you can:

  • Ask a follow-up question for more details
  • Request a simpler explanation if something is confusing
  • Ask for step-by-step instructions if you need guidance

You can also provide feedback by clicking the "This was helpful" or "This wasn't helpful" buttons. Your feedback helps us improve.

Important Things to Know

Trustworthy Information

We strive to provide accurate information, but AI isn't perfect. That's why we show you the sources of our information and clearly indicate when we're uncertain about something.

For critical health or financial decisions, always consult with professionals.

Privacy and Security

Your questions are used to improve our service, but we don't store personal information that could identify you. We don't ask for your name, address, or financial information.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Accessibility Features

Boomer Ask is designed to be easy to use for everyone. You can:

  • Adjust text size using the A+ and A- buttons
  • Use voice input instead of typing
  • Navigate with a keyboard if using a mouse is difficult
